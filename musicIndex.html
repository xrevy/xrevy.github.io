Music Art Settings

The Draw visualization generates strokes, draws, based on the music. Choose a song and see the brush of the music paint its picture.

The triangle visualization shows a triangle that moves based on the music analysis. But its not a always moving triangle, you'll be able to see the past triangle positions aswell. It draws as triangle.

The square visualization creates whilst the song is playing 81 squares. The squares are bigger the louder the part of the song is when it is created. Their colours are also mapped from the music analysis if high, mid and bass frequencies.

Background Settings:
If you choose the dynamic background, each second a part of the background will be generated. The colour of that part is based on the song analysis at that moment.
If you choose the gradient background, you get to choose two colours the background is filled with.
You can choose a single coulor for the background using the color picker.
